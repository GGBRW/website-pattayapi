Social media analysis

It is often the case that people in Thailand will use multiple social networking accounts to conceal their activities. Typically an individual in multiple relationships will use multiple Facebook accounts and multiple e-mail addresses to hide what they are doing. We are able to use forensic techniques to uncover digital footprints. We always recommend social media investigation as a starting point in infidelity investigations. We do not recommend this as a stand alone service as it can never be 100 percent guaranteed - if someone is taking extreme measures to conceal their activity online it may not be possible for any investigator to uncover this. We urge you to be very cautious of companies offering social media analysis, this can provide you with a false sense of security if not coupled with other surveillance work.
